SEVENTEEN

A Korean group who named SEVENTEEN


SEVENTEEN is a South Korean boy group formed by Pledis Entertainment in 2015. There are 13 members, and all the members divided into three sub-unit, which is Hip-Hop Unit, Vocal Unit and Performance Unit. The name SEVENTEEN means 13 members plus 3 units plus one group. The official fanclub name is Carat.
